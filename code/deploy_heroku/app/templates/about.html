What is this app doing?

Based on your rating of movies you have watched, this app recommends 5 movies, that will make your next movie night awesome!!!

How does this app work?

  • This app is built on the small MovieLense dataset, movie poster have been downloaded from movieposterdb.
  • Non-negative matrix factorization (NMF) is used as recommendation engine. The NMF was tuned regarding fitting time and accuracy and works with 5 hidden features . The NMF algorithm of the surprise package has been implemented, since the surprise version allows for NaNs (unlike the scikit-version of NMF).
  • To get an initial user rating for a variety of different movies, 40 movies are displayed on the home screen and can be rated by the user. For this, the movies were clustered into 10 clusters using k-means (based on the hidden NMF-features with prior PCA to get reduce features to 3 dimensions). 4 movies are then randomly selected from each of the 10 clusters, taking popularity into account (i.e. random sampling with popularity as weights).
  • The 5 movies with the highest rating prediction are returned and displayed on the results page.
